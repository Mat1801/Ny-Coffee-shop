Our flagship café takes inspiration from traditional Colombian coffee farms, with a pitched roof, skylight, sunlit courtyard, and handcrafted wooden details that echo the architecture of the campo. Guests enter through an exposed-brick corridor that opens to lush Colombian greenery, immediately immersing them in the world of Devoción. From the street, the new coffee laboratory is visible, an educational space for trainings, cuppings, tastings, and classes open to the public. The redesigned bar area now features two espresso machines for greater efficiency and a smoother guest experience.
